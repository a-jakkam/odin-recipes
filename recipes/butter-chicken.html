Butter Chicken

Description

Butter chicken is a popular Indian dish that is known for its rich and creamy sauce. The dish consists of tender pieces of marinated chicken that are cooked and then simmered in a buttery tomato-based gravy. The sauce is made with a blend of spices such as cumin, coriander, and garam masala, along with cream and butter to give it a smooth and velvety texture. Butter chicken is typically served with rice or naan bread, making it a satisfying and comforting meal.

The origins of butter chicken can be traced back to Delhi, India in the 1950s, where it was created by the chefs at Moti Mahal restaurant. The dish was invented as a way to use leftover tandoori chicken and has since become a staple in Indian cuisine. Butter chicken has gained popularity worldwide for its delicious flavors and comforting qualities, making it a favorite dish for many who enjoy Indian food. Its creamy, buttery sauce and tender chicken make it a decadent and satisfying meal that is perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • Marinated chicken
  • Curd
  • Turmeric
  • Chilli powder
  • Coriander powder
  • Garam masala
  • Tomatoes
  • Kasuri methi

Steps

  1. Heat some oil in a pan and add the marinated chicken. Cook until the chicken is fully cooked. Set aside.
  2. In the same pan, add chopped tomatoes and cook until they are soft and mushy.
  3. Add kasuri methi to the tomatoes and cook for a few minutes.
  4. Add the cooked chicken to the tomato mixture and let it simmer for a few minutes.
  5. Add salt to taste and adjust the seasoning if needed.
  6. Serve hot with rice or naan. Enjoy your homemade butter chicken!
